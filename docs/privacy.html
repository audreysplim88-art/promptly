Privacy Policy

Promptly — Last updated April 2026

Promptly is a browser extension that helps you craft better prompts for AI tools. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

What we collect

When you use Promptly, the goal you type and your answers to the interview questions are sent to our server solely to generate your optimised prompt. We do not store, log, or share this content beyond what is required to return the result to you.

What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, browsing history, or any personal identifiers. We do not track which AI tools you use or what you do after a prompt is generated.

Third-party services

Prompt generation is powered by the Anthropic Claude API. Your prompt text is sent to Anthropic's servers to produce the result. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy for details on how they handle API data.

Data retention

We do not persistently store your prompt content on our servers. Requests are processed in memory and discarded after the response is returned.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/audreysplim88-art/promptly.
